Every Shipment, Delivered with Excellence

Everquick Supply-Chain Management (Guangdong) Co., Ltd., headquartered in Jiangmen, was founded in 2021 with a registered capital of RMB 5 million. We are a professional service provider dedicated to delivering end-to-end supply chain solutions for businesses.

Since our inception, we have upheld the principles of "Efficiency, Reliability, and Transparency," with our business covering supply chain management, international freight forwarding, general cargo warehousing, domestic trade agency, and building materials sales — committed to providing one-stop, comprehensive supply chain services to our clients.

Our team comprises senior supply chain experts, international trade consultants, and digital systems engineers, combining deep industry knowledge with technical capability to truly understand our clients' pain points and deliver actionable solutions.
