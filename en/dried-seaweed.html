Required Import Documents

Essential documents for dried seaweed import clearance with sample references (sensitive info redacted). We provide full document review and agency services.

Commercial Invoice

Issued by the exporter, stating cargo value, country of origin, and trade method. It is the key basis for customs to assess dutiable value.

Sales Contract

The official contract signed by both buyer and seller, specifying product name, quantity, unit price, trade terms (FOB/CIF), and payment method.

Packing List

Details net weight, gross weight, volume, and packaging per shipment unit. Must be consistent with the Bill of Lading and Invoice.

Certificate of Origin

Issued by the exporting country's official or authorized body. Used to claim preferential tariff rates, making it critical for reducing import costs.

Export License

Some source countries require government-issued export licenses for dried seaweed. We help coordinate with suppliers to obtain them in time.

Phytosanitary Certificate

Issued by the exporting country's plant quarantine authority, certifying that the cargo is free from harmful organisms and meets China's entry requirements.

Bill of Lading

Issued by the shipping company as proof of ownership and the basis for cargo release. We verify B/L details to ensure consistency with L/C and other documents.

Health Certificate

Official health certificate issued by the exporting country, confirming the product meets food safety and hygiene standards — essential for customs release.

Import Clearance Process

Full visibility from origin to your warehouse in China

📋

1. Document Review

We collect and review all import documents 15 working days before shipment arrival, identifying issues in advance to ensure compliance.

🚢

2. International Shipping

FCL/LCL ocean freight from the origin port directly to Nansha Port, Guangzhou (Guangdong's primary import port) with full cargo tracking.

🏛️

3. Customs Declaration

Upon arrival, we handle customs declaration, inspection coordination, and duty/VAT payment with our professional brokerage team for fast clearance.

🏭

4. Warehousing & Delivery

After clearance, we arrange trucking for cargo pickup — warehousing with QC or direct delivery to your factory for last-mile fulfillment.
